







Harry Potter is a young boy who discovers on his eleventh birthday that he is a wizard. Raised by his cruel aunt and uncle, Harry learns he belongs to a magical world and attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. At Hogwarts, he makes lifelong friends, faces dangerous enemies, and learns about his mysterious past, including the dark wizard 
Voldemort, who killed his parents. As Harry grows, he embarks on a journey to stop Voldemort and protect the wizarding world from evil.
